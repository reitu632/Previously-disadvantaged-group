About Us

The Previously Disadvantaged Group was established in 2007. It is a community-based organisation that is dedicated to enhancing people's lives and communities.

We drive initiatives such as sponsorship, support programmes, and food supply to advance sustainable development, empowerment, and dignity.

Mission

To empower disadvantaged communities by providing sustainable support programmes, food assistance, sponsorship opportunities, and initiatives that promote growth, dignity, and self-reliance.

Vision

To build thriving, empowered communities where individuals and teams have equal access to opportunities, resources, and the support needed to reach their full potential.
